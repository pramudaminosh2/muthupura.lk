Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities

1. Introduction

Muthupura.lk is a digital vehicle marketplace designed to connect buyers and sellers across Sri Lanka. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

2. Data We Collect

When you use Muthupura.lk, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Your full name, phone number, and email address
  • Vehicle Details: Information about vehicles you list, including make, model, year, price, and photos
  • Account Information: Login credentials and authentication data
  • Transaction Data: Records of your vehicle listings and inquiries

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Display your vehicle listings on our platform
  • Facilitate communication between buyers and sellers
  • Verify user identity and prevent fraud
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send administrative notifications and updates

4. Data Protection & Security

We prioritize your data security. Your personal information is protected using industry-standard encryption and security measures. We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

Muthupura.lk uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Firebase: For authentication, database storage, and hosting
  • Aiven Database: For secure data management and backup

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in to your account
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website usage patterns

You can control cookies through your browser settings at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
